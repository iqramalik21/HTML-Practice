My Favorite Food is Custard. It is Basically a creamy dessert (semi-solid) mainly made of milk, the milk is flavored with custard powder which can be of any flavor for eaxample Strawberry, banana, vanilla or Chocolate etc. Its creamy taste is the main reason why I like it so much.

today I will write the recipe of custard:

Ingredients:

  • Custard Powder
  • One Kg Milk
  • Pot

Recipe:

  1. Boil the Milk in a pot 🍵
  2. Make a Mixture of Custard Powder
    • Take a cup of Milk 🥛
    • Add Custard Powder in it
    • Mix it
  3. Add Custard Powder Mixture in Milk 🥄
  4. Mix it and keep heating it until it becomes viscous
  5. Pour it in Bowl and let it Cool 🥣
  6. Refrigerate it ❄
  7. Voila! Serve it 🍮
